Moodify

Moodify is a modern music web player inspired by Spotify but with a twist. It uses advanced algorithms to suggest and play music based on your mood. Whether you're feeling happy, relaxed, or introspective, Moodify will curate the perfect playlist to match your vibe and enhance your listening experience.
