Welcome to Corebell Engine

Corebell Engine is a lightweight, modular 3D web engine designed for simplicity and performance. Here's what you can and cannot do with it:

What You Can Do:

  • Render 3D objects (cubes, spheres, etc.).
  • Add physics to objects (gravity, collisions).
  • Interact with objects (click, drag, rotate).
  • Load external assets (models, textures).
  • Use a toolbar to add and manipulate objects.

What You Cannot Do (Yet):

  • Multiplayer or networking features.
  • Advanced post-processing effects (e.g., bloom, anti-aliasing).
  • Complex animations or skeletal rigging.
